Who I Am

I'm a passionate creative currently studying Interactive Media at Universidad San Francisco de Quito (USFQ), in my 3rd semester. My journey into the creative world came after spending 2 years studying General Psychology at UISEK, which gave me a unique perspective on user behavior and human-centered design. Since then, I've been honing my skills in UI/UX design, motion graphics, game design, and web development, driven by a love for crafting immersive digital experiences. Aslo a fan of horror games, I enjoy exploring how atmosphere and storytelling can enhance user engagement.
